In 2017, Johnny Lyden and Lina Zoo partnered to build a creative development and design agency to learn, experiment and try new technologies and ideas, thus SEVEN SEAS DIGITAL was born. From wireframing to fast prototyping Seven Seas aims to 'build to fail fast' in a conceptually focused studio. The goal is to deliver and test ideas. Open for comission work on a case-by-case basis, however, do not hesitate to reach out to us!
