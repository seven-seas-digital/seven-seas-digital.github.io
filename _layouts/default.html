WHO ARE WE?

In 2017, Johnny Lyden and Lina Zoo partnered to build an Austin-based development and design creative consultancy SEVEN SEAS DIGITAL. Together we specialize in creative development strategies and production of public spaces online. From wireframing to fast prototyping we build to fail-fast in a conceptually focused studio. Our goal is to deliver and test ideas. We decided to offer our services to people who want to do the same with the ideas in their head. We will help you build experiences and prototypes so you can go from idea to realization quickly. Don't hesitate to contact us, tell us about your project and what you wish it could become.
